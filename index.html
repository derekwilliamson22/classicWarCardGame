See Console For Game...

Directions:

  • Open your browser's Dev Tools (e.g. "Inspect" in Chrome), to access the console.
  • Type "gameOfWar("Name of Player 1", "Name of Player 2")" into the console.
  • For example, gameOfWar("Derek", "Noah")
  • For now, refresh the browser to play again.
  • If the game reaches 2000 rounds, it will end (it might keep going otherwise!).
